What is Digital Confidence?
Digital Confidence is a free, non-profit cybersecurity awareness app designed for elderly people, parents, students, and office workers. Our goal is to help everyday people stay safe online by learning to recognize scams, fraud, and digital threats.

What you'll learn:
• How to spot fake calls and messages
• How to protect your bank and passwords
• How to recognize phishing emails
• How to stay safe on social media
• Real-life scam scenarios and how to handle them
